Rules!





How the game works


The game starts with all the cards face down and the player takes turns to turn over two cards. If the two cards have the same picture, then the cards stay facing up, otherwise the cards face down again. The player wins when cards when all cards are matched and facing upwards.



How to play



To play the game, click two cards on the screen to flip them over. If they match, the cards will remain facing upright. Continue this process until you win!



How do lives work?


Lives represent remaining attempts at matching cards. Each incorrect match will result in a life being removed. When the total amount of lives reaches 0, the game board will reset and all cards positions will be randomised.
